What is LinkForge?

LinkForge is a powerful and intuitive URL shortening service designed to transform long, unwieldy web addresses into short, memorable, and trackable links. Whether you're sharing content on social media, in emails, or through marketing campaigns, LinkForge provides the tools you need to streamline your online presence.

How It Works

The process is simple. Take any long URL, paste it into our shortener, and we'll forge a new, compact link for you.

For example, a long link like this:

https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08C76W234/ref=sspa_dk_detail_0?psc=1&pd_rd_i=B08C76W234...

Becomes a short and clean link like this:

https://linkforge.io/aB3xZ9c

Key Features

  • Fast & Reliable Shortening: Instantly create short links for any URL.
  • Click Tracking: Monitor how many clicks your links receive over time.
  • QR Code Generation: Automatically generate a scannable QR code for every link, perfect for print media and physical displays.
  • Secure & Private: Manage your links from your personal dashboard, securely tied to your account.
